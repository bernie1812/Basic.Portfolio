About Me

I was born on January 27, 1971 in St. Louis, MO. I am the middle child of three boys. I have great memories of my childhood. Family vacations, summer camp and a great group of friends.


I attended Horton Watkins High School. While there I participated in after school activities such as sports and the Big Brother Big Sister Program.


Upon graduation I attended the University of Mississippi and earned a Bachelor's Degree in Business Management.
